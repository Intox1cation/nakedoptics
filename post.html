What are polarising lenses good for?

Have you ever heard of polarised lenses? With polarised glasses, the lenses are equipped with a polarising filter (polar filter) that minimises glare and ensures that you see contrasts and colours better and that the rays reach the eye weakened. Polarised sports glassesare especially recommended for activities in the water or on the mountain, where you are exposed to different light conditions. But how does the polfiter work? When light shining in different directions hits a flat surface, the light rays are reflected horizontally and vertically. The vertical light rays are then absorbed by the polarising filter.
