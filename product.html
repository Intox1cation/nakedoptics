The FALCON Sport Glasses

NAKED Optics stands for high-quality and affordable ski goggles & snowboard goggles with mirrored lenses. New models are developed in Austria in cooperation with athletes and ski instructors and are thus adapted to the needs of winter sports enthusiasts.

The magnetic system of the interchangeable lenses is also special. This ensures perfect vision even in fog and snowfall without fogging up the ski goggles & snowboard goggles. Thanks to the unique design of the models, people who wear glasses will also find what they are looking for in the range (OTG).

A team is responsible for the popular styles and thus provides the finishing touches to the ski goggles and snowboard goggles. Freeriders and freestylers not only appreciate the advantages of NAKED Optics in Austria, but also in Sweden, Japan, New Zealand and Chile.
